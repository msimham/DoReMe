PK (str) user_id - person's username
(str) first_name - person's first name
(str) last_name - person's last name
(int) age - person's age
(list of str) genres - list of genres person is interested (pop, rnb, country, rap, hip hop, indie, etc)
(list of str) roles - list of roles person is qualified for (singer, lyricist, composer, producer, pianist, drummer, dancer, etc)
(list of str) skill_proficiency - skill proficiency corresponding to role (beginner, intermediate, advanced, expert)
(list of str) skill_engagement - engagement level corresponding to role (hobbyist, serious hobbyist, semi-professional, professional)
(str) location - person's current location
(boolean) ok_not_local - person ok to relocation
(float) weekly_time - amount of time ok to commit per week
(boolean) considers_age - person considering age for collab or not 
(int) age_limit - person +/- this many years ok with
